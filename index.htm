The plastic bokeh effects for cameras: The latest revolution produced by Thai creators!

The only shaped bokeh creating product available in stores to ease the excitements and creations in bokeh photography.

Easily by attaching it onto a camera lens sized 52mm. or a camera filter. It is very easy to assemble by just a small push on the effect to the lens and it will neatly be attached without any troubles of twisting or falling off.

Its edge is designed to make attaching and detaching easy without any scratches on the lens and control the degrees of turning to position the bokeh shape for user’s flavors. The most recommended suitable lenses to use it are “Canon 50 f1.8” and “Nikon 50 f1.8”.

The mechanism of a bokeh picture is when a camera takes a picture from an out-of-focus ray that passes through its lens. It happens when a picture is taken from a low focus condition or through the wide light receiving camera, which will make the blur light spots on the picture.

By all mean it depends on the lens, the position of the model and the distance of the background lights. So to take a beautiful bokeh picture the bokeh effect must be attached, the model really has to be around focal point and positioned at quite far from the background lights to create big light shapes on the background of the picture.
